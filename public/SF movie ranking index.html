Steffen's top movies of 2024

Oppenheimer

Oppenheimer is an American biographical film directed by Christopher Nolan, who wrote the screenplay with Robert. The film stars Cillian Murphy as J. Robert Oppenheimer, alongside Emily Blunt, Matt Damon, Robert Downey Jr., and Florence Pugh. The film depicts the life of the theoretical physicist J. Robert Oppenheimer, who is best known for his role as the director of the Los Alamos Laboratory during World War II, where he led the effort to develop the first nuclear weapons. The film is produced by Syncopy and distributed by Universal Pictures.


Barbie

Barbie is an American musical comedy film directed by Greta Gerwig, who wrote the screenplay with Noah Baumbach. The film stars Margot Robbie as Barbie, alongside Ryan Gosling, Brad Pitt, and Meryl Streep. The film is based on the Barbie toy line, and follows Barbie as she discovers that she doesn't fit into the perfect land of Barbies and journeys to the real world, where she discovers that being unique is an asset. The film is produced by LuckyChap Entertainment and distributed by Warner Bros. Pictures.


The Hunger games: The Ballad 0f Songbirds & Snakes

The Hunger Games: The Ballad of Songbirds and Snakes is an American science fiction action-adventure film based on Suzanne Collins's novel of the same name. The film is directed by Francis Lawrence and written by Michael Arndt. It stars Dylan O'Brien, Lily Collins, and Jonny Weston. The film is a prequel to The Hunger Games film series, and is set 64 years before the events of the first film. The film is produced by Color Force and distributed by Lionsgate Films.
